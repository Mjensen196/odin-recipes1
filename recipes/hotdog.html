Hotdog

Classic hotdog

In this recipe we will be making a classic hotdog

Ingredients we will need

  • Poppy seed bun
  • A hotdog
  • Pickle spear
  • Onion
  • Mustard

How to make this beaut

  1. Boil or grill your hotdog until your preferred stage
  2. Once done stick it onto your poppy seed bun
  3. Slap your pickle spear down onto the meat
  4. Drop some onions down on her
  5. Smother the hotdog in mustard and call it a day
