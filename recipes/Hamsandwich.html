Ham Sandwich

A delicious sandwich

In this recipe you will learn how to make a very delicious sandwich. The ham sandwich is an american staple, and has been around since the age of cavemen.

Ingredients we will need

  • Sesame bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayonnaise
  • Preffered cheese
  • Any ham

How to make the sucker

  1. Put your sesame bread in the toaster on a medium setting
  2. Prepare your toppings on a plate while the bread toasts
  3. Once the bread is done put a slice of cheese on each slice of bread to seal the sandwiches juices from the toasted bread
  4. Put a few slices of lettuce on the cheese, followed by a slice or two of tomato
  5. Take a fat slab of ham and slap it down on one half of the bread, then smother it in mayonnaise
  6. Combine both halves and enjoy
